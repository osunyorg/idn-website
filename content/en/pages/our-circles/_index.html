meta_description: >- summary: >- The IDN is organized in circles that link with each other by having two individuals in each circle (the arrows). We have Working Group Circles (in green) that are organized by theme and Continental Circles (in red) that are organized by geographical region. We also have the Organising Circle (in blue), which helps the internal functioning of the IDN. Lastly, the General Circle (in purple) bring all the circles together and decides the strategy and aims of the IDN. Some Circles even have sub-circles to split up work into smaller teams! header_text: >- header_cta: display: false label: >- target: "" external: false contents_reading_time: seconds: 10 text: >- less than a minute contents: - kind: block template: chapter title: >- ranks: self: false data: layout: no_background text: >-
