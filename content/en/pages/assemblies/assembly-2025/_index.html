The 6th International Assembly of the Degrowth Movement will take place on the 23rd of June 2025 in Olso, Norway and online. There will also be pre-assemblies online which you can already contribute to! See more information below. 

body: >-

Overview

This year, the Assembly will involve three key phases:
An ongoing pre-assembly feedback phase (Feb–March 2025) using a global survey to gather diverse perspectives to shape the foreseen assembly sessions.
A time-zone-sensitive online assembly/ies (April 2025) with interactive sessions to share needs, best practices, and strategies for degrowth.
An in-person assembly (June 23, 2025, Oslo), which will be a full-day event to further connect and collaborate in response to the needs and strategies identified during the online assembly, as well as celebrate diverse community achievements.

The 11th International Degrowth Conference and the 18th Conference of the International Society for Ecological Economics will take place after the Assembly. Find out more!

Pre-Assembly Survey

The first
phase is open, and you can contribute to the survey until March 2nd. We will
use its results to prepare the assembly sessions accordingly.

This is the
survey in English, and we
also have it available in Spanish, in
Portuguese, and in Chinese.

The working
group that organises the 2025 Assembly looks forward to your answers!
