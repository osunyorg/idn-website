Why Join the IDN?

Why Join the IDN?

Networking and Collaboration: Join a global platform to exchange information and best practices with other degrowth organizations, inspiring and supporting each other to achieve larger collective goals.

Increased Visibility: Enhance the visibility of your organization within the international degrowth community, gaining recognition and support.

Shared Resources and Knowledge: Access a wealth of resources, research, and insights from a diverse range of degrowth organizations to strengthen your initiatives and projects.

How to join?

In order for a group to become a member of the IDN, it would need to do the following:
Fill out the form below
Agree the aims and principles of the IDN
Appoint at least one delegate to become an active member of an IDN Circle that will commit to:

Engage in transparent internal communication with other members using our Matrix/Element space.
Contribute to the best of your abilities to an IDN Circle that also aligns with your organisation's goals.


If you have any questions about becoming a member, please contact the Membership and Onboarding Circle join[at]degrowth.net
