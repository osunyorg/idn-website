Why Join the IDN?

  • Networking and Collaboration: Join a global platform to exchange information and best practices with other degrowth organizations, inspiring and supporting each other to achieve larger collective goals.
  • Increased Visibility: Enhance the visibility of your organization within the international degrowth community, gaining recognition and support.
  • Shared Resources and Knowledge: Access a wealth of resources, research, and insights from a diverse range of degrowth organizations to strengthen your initiatives and projects.


In order for a group to become a member of the IDN it should agree to the following:

  1. Follow the aims (external link) and principles (external link) of the IDN
  2. Appoint at least one delegate to become an active member of an IDN Circle (external link)that will commit to:
    • Engage in transparent internal communication with other IDN Circle members through an agreed upon platform (The IDN currently uses Matrix/Element (external link)for this purpose.)
    • Contribute to at least 2 hours per week to an IDN Circle (external link)
