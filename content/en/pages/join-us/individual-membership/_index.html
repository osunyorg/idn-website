body: >-

Why Join the IDN?

Community and Support: Become part of a vibrant, like-minded community passionate about degrowth, where you can exchange ideas and receive support for your initiatives at a global level.

Personal Growth and Learning: Gain more direct access to a wealth of knowledge, discussions, and events that will deepen your understanding of degrowth and help you develop new skills and knowledge.

Active Contribution: Contribute to the growing impact of the degrowth movement by participating in various projects and activities, making a tangible difference in promoting sustainable and equitable living.
The following links can tell you more about our aims and principles that past and existing members have helped to shape.

Become an active member of an IDN Circle!

If you have the privilege of having some spare time that you would like to dedicate to help shape and guide the IDN or just bring the degrowth movement to your community we encourage you to fill out the form below. Once filled, you will receive an invitation to join the IDN Matrix/Element space where members engage in transparent internal communication and will be added to our IDN newsletter. If you have any questions about becoming a member, please contact info[at]degrowth.net  

Sign up form:
Not ready to be a member?

If membership seems to much, you can sign up our newsletter to receive updates on degrowth initiatives, resources, and opportunities to get involved:)

Sign up here!

Want to join as an organisation?

If you are part of an organization that would like to join IDN, please visit our Group & Organisation Membership page. You may also want to browse the degrowth database which contains hundreds of degrowth groups that you contact directly to see how to get involved with them.
