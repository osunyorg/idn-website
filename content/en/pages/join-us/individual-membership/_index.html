Why Join the IDN?

  • Community and Support: Become part of a vibrant, like-minded community passionate about degrowth, where you can exchange ideas and receive support for your initiatives at a global level.
  • Personal Growth and Learning: Gain more direct access to a wealth of knowledge, discussions, and events that will deepen your understanding of degrowth and help you develop new skills and knowledge.
  • Active Contribution: Contribute to the growing impact of the degrowth movement by participating in various projects and activities, making a tangible difference in promoting sustainable and equitable living.

 See here the full IDN aims and principles that past and existing members have help to shape.
  

Become an active member of our General, Working or Continental IDN Circles. (external link)

If you have some time and you would like to dedicate it to helping shape the IDN and bring the degrowth movement to all corners of society, then you should join by completing the form below, signaling which areas you are interested in. 

You will receive an invitation to join IDN Matrix/Element (external link) where members engage in transparent internal communication and will be added to our IDN newsletter.

If you have any questions about becoming a member, please contact join[at]degrowth.net  

Join as an organization:  If you are part of an organization that would like to join IDN, you are welcome to visit Group & Organisation Membership (external link). You may also want to browse this database (external link) of hundreds of degrowth groups and contact them directly to see how to get involved with them.
