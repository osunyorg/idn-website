How to participate?

Here are some examples of what you could do:
Organise a demonstration for a local social or environmental struggle, voicing your demands to communities, municipalities, and governments.
Host a workshop on degrowth and its intersection with a topic of local relevance or interest.
Throw a party to celebrate the achievements of the degrowth community and to raise awareness of our struggle.
Host a discussion on the socio-ecological impacts of endless economic growth and how they appear in your local area (for example, the privatisation of natural or the building of a mega infrastructure project)
Facilitate a gathering to discuss a pressing issue in your local context from a critical perspective and co-design actions for local advocacy.
Or simply gather for a picnic and conviviality! 

Everything goes!
