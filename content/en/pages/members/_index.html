IDN Collaborators

IDN collaborators are a wide range of businesses, collectives, coops, associations and organisations. They may not be aligned with all degrowth principles, but they work with the IDN on everything from facilitating research projects and building post-growth networks to integrating degrowth ideas into their respective operations. A great way for any entity to dip their toe into degrowth. Interested in becoming a collaborator? Contact us at info[at]degrowth.net
