  In 2026, the International Degrowth Network once again invites the degrowth community to apply for a fund dedicated to supporting degrowth events. With the support of the DG Support Group, we aim to distribute funding to regional and thematic initiatives that promote and strengthen degrowth principles. Visit this page to learn more about the fund and how to apply.  

body: >-

Context

Last year the IDN's Events Circle was given responsibility for coordinating the fund distribution in the spirit of increased transparency, democracy, and participation. With additional support from the Support Group and Steady State Manchester, we were able to raise and distribute over €13,000 to 15 groups out of 54 applicants from nearly 30 different countries. Building on this momentum, the fund will continue in 2026 with a total of  €15,000  available to support new regional and thematic degrowth events. We hope to receive even more applications and support a growing number of projects as the degrowth community continues to expand and organize across regions.  Check below for further information.Application process - 

Application process – OPEN NOW till 19th April

To share how we will approach the 2026 Events Fund, we've made our  Requirements for Applications  (external link)  available, which walks through who could apply, timelines, how decisions are made, and what we asked from organisers after their events.  Please, READ IT CAREFULLY!! We've also published the  criteria  (external link)   we use - a living set of priorities shaped collectively within the Events Circle - to help ensure the funds were shared fairly, reached underrepresented regions/people, and supported events that truly reflect degrowth values.  If interested, just apply directly filling up this form  https://my.liberaforms.org/degrowth-events-fund-2026 (external link)   If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions, please do not hesitate to reach us at info[at]degrowth.network
