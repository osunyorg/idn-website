body: >-


The Fund set out to distribute

€

15,000 among small Degrowth-focused events around the world, with a maximum of

€

1,000 per event. We received 34 applications, four of which were immediately rejected because their overall budget was above the limit or because they received money from the Support Group this year. That left us with 31 applications, which our team of nine volunteers evaluated using the previously published criteria . Each event was reviewed by three evaluators, the scores averaged, and the events ranked by this score. Now that the selection is over, and after communicating the results to the participants and preparing our documents for publication, we are finally able to publicly announce the results. The list of events chosen for funding is:

The Balochistan Degrowth Jirga: Reclaiming Indigenous Commons & Ecological Care , Youth Association for Development (YAD) Pakistan

The Soul of Nairobi: Reimagining the City Together , NtuLea Africa

World Social Forum - Financialisation of Life: Resisting False Solutions , Debt for Climate (D4C)

Energy beyond growth in Greece: from extraction to autonomy , Energy Resistance Autonomia - Greece

fruits of our labor: rural degrowth symposium , Ekopraxis Pilipinas

Anti.Oppression Workshops , Decrecimientos Mx

Rethinking Youth Employment in Kenya: A Degrowth Dialogue , green students initiative

JTI Slow Conversations: "All we need is growth, or not?" , Just Transition Indonesia
