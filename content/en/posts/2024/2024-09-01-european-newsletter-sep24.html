Austria:

  • 28th July - 2nd August in Viena https://www.altshiftfestival.org/

This festival was a complete success! Over 140 participants made their way to Lower Austria and co-created 6 days packed with political education, creative sessions, delicious vegan meals and diverse nighttime entertainment. Representatives from more than 15 groups covering diverse social and ecological struggles made their way to AltShift this year to help bridge the gap between degrowth and its sibling movements and create alliances. Check out this short documentary that journalist Méli Bry made about this years Alt Shift festival: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Y-a91yhAakE
