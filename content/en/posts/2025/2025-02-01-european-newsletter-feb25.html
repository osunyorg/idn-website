  • PhD/postdoc in Energy Law and degrowth

Romain Mauger, Head of Legal Research Unit at the Iberian Centre for Research in Energy Storage, Cáceres, Spain is looking for 4 PhD candidates and 1 postdoc to join the team he is setting up for the ERC project DELaw: Degrowth through Energy Law: Using the legal framework for electricity batteries in Europe to achieve a degrowth-focused energy law.
The 4 PhD candidates in Law will study the relevant national regime (about batteries, raw materials and degrowth) in various EU countries (Eastern, Western, Northern and Southern Europe).
In addition, the postdoc should have a PhD from a field of social sciences (broadly understood) but the key requirement is to hold proven knowledge about Degrowth theory (or closely related ones). All positions are for 4 years and to be undertaken on site, in the beautiful city of Cáceres, Spain (with 2 work-from-home days/week). The image below provides more information about the application itself (deadline, where to find the position, etc.).
Robert’s hope is to set up a team of bright minds for this project that contributes at its own scale to the Degrowth movement. He can be reached at romain.mauger@ciiae.org (external link)

Support Degrowthuary! [Europe]

GOOG Berlin is a group of people committed to helping bring about socio-ecological transformation by advocating for degrowth ideas, values, and policies. We’re excited to introduce you to Degrowthuary. To make it a success, we need your help!

Degrowthuary is a social media campaign designed to spread knowledge about degrowth and encourage people to take concrete actions to move towards resource-conscious lifestyles and join the degrowth movement.

Our mission is to make degrowth fun and practical for a wide audience. The campaign will combine clear, engaging content on degrowth with simple daily challenges, proving that shifting our lifestyle is easier than we think. With humor, engaging videos, and real-life tips, we aim to reach eco-conscious people who haven’t yet discovered degrowth—or just need a simple guide to living with less.

The campaign will run throughout February, starting from 1st February, on Instagram and via our newsletter.

We need your help to share the campaign with your followers and/or members. You can:

  • Follow us on Instagram: @degrowthuary_2025
  • Share our first Instagram post (https://www.instagram.com/p/DFFdDVyOl3L/ (external link)) and encourage your audience to participate in the campaign
  • Repost the campaign’s posts, stories, and reels
  • Inform your audience about the campaign and invite them to sign up for our mailing list (http://eepurl.com/i7ExiY (external link)) via your newsletter.

Agroecology Europe Forum 2025, October 2nd-4th in Malmö [Sweden]

The co-creation call of Agroecology Europe Forum 2025 is open to proposals the 1st February - the 31st of March.

MiljöMatematik welcomes you to an information webinar and a facilitation workshop, to support the development of co-created programme sessions for Agroecology Europe Forum 2025.

Agroecology Europe Forum 2025 (external link) is a landmark event shaping the future of European food systems through the power of agroecology! Under the theme of “Transformation in Action!”, the Agroecology Europe Forum 2025 will spotlight collaboration, integration and bold action to accelerate agroecological transitions across Europe. We are bringing together diverse stakeholders to energise, strengthen and expand the European agroecology movement. Share knowledge and experience, gain inspiration, showcase success stories and tackle challenges in a visionary space filled with innovative ideas and actionable solutions. For the first time, the 5th Agroecology Europe Forum is hosted in the Nordics, transforming into a vibrant festival welcoming both food system changemakers and the public. We want everyone to have a taste of the transformation that agroecology offers!

We now welcome food system actors from all sectors with or without explicit linkage to - or previous knowledge of - agroecology to co-create the programme for Agroecology Europe Forum 2025. Are you a farmer, advocate, student, policymaker, researcher, chef, distributor, advisor, retailer, food producer or food-enthusiast in another way? Do you work in alignment with the principles of agroecology? Do you have a wish to contribute? Join us!

  • Co-creation information webinar (online) Date: 3rd of February, 14:00-15:30 Location: Zoom - register for invitation Register here (external link)

  • Co-creation facilitation workshop (on-site) Date: 6th of February, 13:00-16:00 Location: Färjeterminalen, Malmö, Sweden Register here (external link) OBS: limited availability.
