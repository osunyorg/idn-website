Based on tens of responses to our recent survey about creating a space for degrowth researchers to connect, share ideas and support one another

responses, we're inviting you to the  first Online MeetUp of the Research Circle. We are excited to see such strong interest in building this community! It will be specifically dedicated to fostering connection and collaboration among researchers working on topics related to degrowth.   Date:  June 2nd Time:  12:00-13.30 pm CEST Link:  https://meet.google.com/qsa-mvpx-bga Everyone is welcome! In this online gathering, we'll get to know each other and use a collaborative  whiteboard  to explore the challenges we face as degrowth researchers, projects or organizations. You are welcome to start adding your thoughts to the board ahead of time! We're looking forward to meeting you on June 2nd and exchanging ideas on how to support each other and advance degrowth research. Bring a friend!  If you have any questions about the meeting, the Research Circle, or IDN, don't hesitate to reach out. Katharina, João and Oxana on behalf of the IDN's Research Circle

Based on tens of responses to our recent survey about creating a space for degrowth researchers to connect, share ideas and support one another responses, we're inviting you to the first Online MeetUp of the Research Circle.

We are excited to see such strong interest in building this community!

It will be specifically dedicated to fostering connection and collaboration among researchers working on topics related to degrowth. 
Date: June 2nd
Time: 12:00-13.30 pm CEST
Link: https://meet.google.com/qsa-mvpx-bga (external link)
Everyone is welcome!

In this online gathering, we'll get to know each other and use a collaborative whiteboard (external link) to explore the challenges we face as degrowth researchers, projects or organizations.

You are welcome to start adding your thoughts to the board ahead of time! We're looking forward to meeting you on June 2nd and exchanging ideas on how to support each other and advance degrowth research.

Bring a friend! If you have any questions about the meeting, the Research Circle, or IDN, don't hesitate to reach out.

Katharina, João and Oxana on behalf of the IDN's Research Circle
