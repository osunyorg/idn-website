European Circle update

Our last circle meeting was a relaxed one, where we shared some reflections on the Oslo conference. If interested in more detail you can read the  Outcomes  from the  Assembly of the Degrowth movement  or check the  Recordings  of the  ISEE-Degrowth conference. We continued taking small steps in the organisation of our webinar series coming in Autumn. Remember the preliminary dates and topics (to be confirmed with partners and speakers):
Thursday September 18th: Rights of nature
Wednesday October 14th: Degrowth policies
Monday November 10th: The imperial mode of living
Friday December 5th: Collapse awareness
If this sounds interesting, keep your eyes and ears open for future updates! We are also still looking to select new role-holders (coordinator, representative, secretary), so do feel warmly invited to our next Circle meeting on Wednesday August 13th, 4:30 - 5:30 PM CEST,  here . The minutes of the past European Circle meetings can still be found  here .  

👋 New IDN members

SERI - Sustainable Europe Research Institute Deutschland e.V. SERI Germany (founded 2005) is an independent, not-for-profit, public-interest research institute for sustainable development. Its fellows cover a rich variety of disciplines from natural and social science to the humanities. They draw on this expertise to contribute fit-for-purpose knowledge, ideas, and tools to collaborations and public debate, typically on a European level. 🌐  SERI Website

📣 Calls for help!

PostGrowth.co The postgrowth.co collective exists to support anyone who wants to work or get involved with the postgrowth movement. In their own words: " It is about not being afraid to ask for help if you need it - and offering help to others freely, without any kind of expectations ". If this resonates, check out the PostGrowth.co website  and maybe even become a member (it is free!) Would you like input for a project, have some news or a job opportunity to share or anything else you need help with from our degrowth community, let us know at  europe@degrowth.net !
