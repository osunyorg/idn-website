We focus on degrowth research and advocacy in Taiwan. We believe it is crucial to develop local degrowth structures and strategies suited to the Taiwanese context. Therefore, we not only study international cases but also conduct research and promote degrowth practices within Taiwan’s civil society. So far, we have established a study club and action projects, including local field research and public outreach activities.
