Within the network, we use an email list to share information, keep in touch and arrange meetings. Send us an e-mail at kontakt@vekstfri.no with a few words about yourself, why you are interested and what you would like to contribute to the network in order to sign up for the e-mail list. When registering, you also accept that we can share your name and e-mail address with the other people in the network, in order to comply with the Norwegian Data Protection Authority's guidelines on privacy considerations.

Degrowth Norway is a network collaborating on transformative changes in Norway, to enable emancipation from capitalism and the growth ideology. We democratically question what kind of future we want, and how changes can happen with respect to both nature and people, in a global perspective with justice and solidarity.
