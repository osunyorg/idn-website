We're a grassroots activist group that aims to raise awareness of the harms of Big Tech, and to help push people onto more ethical options.
We're building an online community of like-minded people trying to shift away from Big Tech, supporting them throughout this process, as well as working to target schools and educational facilities to help educate them and shift their users away from Big Tech.
