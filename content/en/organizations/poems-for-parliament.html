'Poems for Parliament' is a publishing house. The sole author is me, Barbara Williams. All publications are free and relate to matters that connect with peaceful and equitable economic Degrowth. I confer with a group of 50+ colleagues who are members of the Degrowthers Google email group. We share research papers and opinions.
