Founded in 2024 from the grassroots collective “Tokyo Snails,” Degrowth Japan unites academics, artists, farmers, and activists to challenge growth-driven economics. Embracing slowness, simplicity, and sufficiency, the group formalized amid rising ecological urgency to expand its impact while preserving its grassroots, post-growth philosophy.
