Tribe-X is an NGO working to decolonize technology through promoting digital autonomy and degrowth. We organize conferences, workshops, and migration sessions helping people move away from Big Tech toward ethical, low-impact alternatives. Our work combines political education, hands-on support, and community building around decentralized, accessible, and non-extractive technologies.
