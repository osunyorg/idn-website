notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- ---

Benvenuti in Italia stems from a network of associative experiences that have contributed to promoting democracy, solidarity, active citizenship and anti-mafia in Italy, in the tradition of democratic and reformist thought. It aims to create interest around projects and ideas developed by a specialised think tank, and then proceed to persuade public opinion and pressure decision-makers through a dedicated advocacy team.
