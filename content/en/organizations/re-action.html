The Re-Action Collective works with the IDN’s Practice Circle to promote businesses in the outdoor industry that already apply degrowth principles. A joint presentation will be held at the Oslo International Degrowth Conference in June 2025 and at the Next Economies conference in Istanbul in September 2025. Re-Action also actively supports the academic works of IDN members studying for master's and PhDs related to degrowth in business.
