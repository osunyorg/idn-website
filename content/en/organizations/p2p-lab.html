P2P Lab is an interdisciplinary research collective focused on the commons. We employ participatory and community-based methods to develop insights on open-source technologies and practices; write, edit and publish articles, reports, and books on diverse topics; organise events for action, reflection and engagement with critical and creative tools for society-changing.
