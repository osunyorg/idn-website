Kreisler is a neighbourhood-based repair and sharing space that puts sufficiency, care, and collective use before consumption. People repair household items, exchange everyday skills, and organise around shared needs, extending product lifetimes, reducing waste, and building local solidarity, self-organisation, and low-threshold alternatives to resource-intensive lifestyles.
