Our mission is to connect people, ideas, and actions to drive Denmark’s shift to a circular economy through creating community resilience. We bring organisations and citizens together through practical knowledge and hands-on events/workshops that make circularity practical and actionable. We envision a Denmark where circular thinking is an ongoing conversation, embedded in how we design, produce and consume. Our mission supports a future where organisations and citizens work together to create systems that prioritise sustainability, community resilience, and shared responsibility.
