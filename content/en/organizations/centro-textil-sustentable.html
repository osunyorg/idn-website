The Sustainable Textile Center (CTS) aimed to integrate social and environmental aspects into textiles/fashion. Faced with the failure of sustainability (the Western system), it began to delve deeper into degrowth. In March 2026, it will present a book on "Rights of Nature and Fashion" and, also in 2026, will teach a program on "Fashion and Degrowth."
