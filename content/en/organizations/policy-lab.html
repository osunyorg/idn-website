Policy Lab is a Slovenian think-and-act tank advancing degrowth through research, education, and political action. We organize the Degrowth Political School, create spaces for critical dialogue, and connect movements to promote systemic change toward a just, democratic, and ecologically sustainable society beyond the growth imperative.
