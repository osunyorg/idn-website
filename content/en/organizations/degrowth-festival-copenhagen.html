DEGROWTH FESTIVAL, Copenhagen. This festival aims to create space for collaborative rethinking around Degrowth. It embraces a patchwork of conversations between laymen, scholars, activists, artists and just anyone curious and interested in being part of remodelling our society. the Syndicate of Creatures organizing the festival is an interdisciplinary art and intervention collective based in Copenhagen.
