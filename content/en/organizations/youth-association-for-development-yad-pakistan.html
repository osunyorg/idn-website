The Youth Association for Development (YAD) empowers youth and marginalized communities in Baluchistan, Pakistan through advocacy, education, and capacity-building. YAD focuses on social development, health, climate justice, and human rights, leveraging digital tools, community engagement, and partnerships to promote inclusion, leadership, and sustainable development
