notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- ---

FYEG is the European umbrella organisation for Young Green organisations from all over the continent, from Azerbaijan to Ireland and from Cyprus to Norway. Our member organisations comprise various backgrounds: local ecological groups, student unions, youth wings of Green parties and Green Youth NGOs. In addition, we are also the independent, official youth wing of the European Green Party (EGP).
