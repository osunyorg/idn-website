SERI Germany (founded 2005) is an independent, not-for-profit, public-interest research institute for sustainable development. Its fellows cover a rich variety of disciplines from natural and social science to the humanities. We draw on this expertise to contribute fit-for-purpose knowledge, ideas, and tools to collaborations and public debate, typically on a European level.
