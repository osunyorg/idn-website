Our organization works at the intersection of green skills development, green jobs creation, and the green economy. We design training programs, leadership pathways, and applied learning opportunities that equip young people with market-relevant green skills—enabling them to access employment, start green enterprises, and contribute to sustainable economic growth.

Our vision
We envision an Africa where young people are not beneficiaries of climate programs, but skilled contributors to a thriving green economy—driving innovation, productivity, and inclusive growth.
