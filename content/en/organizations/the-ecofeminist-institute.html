The Ecofeminist Institute is an organization that helps shape a world where climate justice, gender equality, minority rights, and environmental sustainability are not just lofty ideals but central tenets of policy-making and business strategies. We provide research, education, and consultancy services to promote equality in climate policies and a fairer transition towards more sustainable futures.
