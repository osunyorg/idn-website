An international forum of individuals who share degrowth values and a degrowth vision.

Three main activity areas:
EDUCATION - academic panels and sharing educational content on social media channels.
ACTIVISM - strategy and actions, building networks of support, knowledge sharing and collaboration events around degrowth-related struggles.
PRACTICE - sensory workshops and field trips.
