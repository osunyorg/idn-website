ShiftSlow is an association for the promotion of a just and colourful social-ecological transformation, which was founded in 2021 in Vienna, Austria. ShiftSlow’s purpose is to provide critical education around the topics of post- or degrowth, development strategies, cultural change and a global socio-ecological transition towards a good life for all.
