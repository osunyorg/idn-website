The Norbert Elias Center researches how social change can succeed in the face of ecological, social, and political crises—with the goals of sustainability and global justice. Our work combines critical transformation research with the analysis of historical, present, and future developments, as well as the participatory design of systemic change—in close cooperation with academia, civil society, and social movements.
