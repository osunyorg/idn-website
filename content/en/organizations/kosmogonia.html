Through a 2 years international trip from France to India (2023-2025), we are conducting qualitative interviews with pioneers and active members engaged in ecological and degrowth initiatives (new economic models, education, NGOs, institutions, collectives...) and also with people who had a singular ecological journey of life. The purpose is to develop a large action-research sociological program about the ingredients that promote ecological shifts for people (we are here interested in radical individual and collective changes and not just pro-ecological behaviour). We want to create bridges between the research universe and concrete transformations.
