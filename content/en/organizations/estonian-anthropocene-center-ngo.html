Biotoopia is a citizens’ initiative in Estonia that seeks to build networks between the arts, sciences and biotic communities. Biotoopia amplifies the power of multispecies communities by shifting patterns of thought and behaviour in order to sustain a flourishing future for all. It is in the symbiosis of the arts and sciences with the biosphere, in the exciting frontier areas, that a deeper understanding can emerge that will help us open perspectives for life itself.
