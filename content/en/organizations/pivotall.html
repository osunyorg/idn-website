At PIVOTALL (pi·vuh·tl), we are working to pivot from "business-as-usual"  to "business-that-benefits" all. 

We help local “Main Street“ businesses find real, practical ways to do right by their people, their communities, and the planet.

Degrowth businesses (DBs) focus on making life better without doing harm.
