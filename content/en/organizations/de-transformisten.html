The Transformists strive for a society where everyone can live well and with quality that is within the limits of people and the planet. We question overconsumption and overproduction and push for an economy of enough. Our campaigns and activities encourage the longer use, reuse, repair and sharing of things. We work in tandem with our sister organization, Repair&Share.
