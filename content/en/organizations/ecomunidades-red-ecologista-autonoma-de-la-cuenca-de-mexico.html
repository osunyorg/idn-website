An ecologist network founded in 2005 as a refoundation of the Foro Regional Ecologista del Valle de México (1991) dedicated to the defence of the soil, water and air of Mexico Valley Bassin. Combating excessive use of transportation, pipes and pumps, and paving and the excessive generation of garbage and waste. Creating alliances with neighbourhood movements against megaprojects. Making proposals for descrecimiento.
