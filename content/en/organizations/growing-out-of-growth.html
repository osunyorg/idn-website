Growing out of Growth (GOOG) is a group of people striving to help bring about socio-ecological transformations by advocating for degrowth/post-growth ideas, values and policies. We aim to spread knowledge about degrowth in person and through social media, build an inclusive and caring community, connect with other movements and impact local policy-making.
